Parmesan garlic pizza rolls

This Parmesan garlic pizza rolls recipe quickly adds a lot of flavor to convenient frozen pizza rolls.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 (24.8-ounce) package pizza rolls, any flavor
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced, or more to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Prepare pizza rolls in the air fryer according to package directions.
  3. Meanwhile, heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add pizza rolls to pan and toss to coat. Add Parmesan cheese and toss to coat. Serve warm.
